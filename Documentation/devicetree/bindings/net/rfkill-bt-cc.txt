* RFKILL of Digi ConnectCore Devices (rfkill-bt-cc driver)

Required properties:

- compatible : Should comply with the format "rfkill-bt-cc"
- vin-supply : Regulator for supplying voltage to Bluetooth chip

Example:

bluetooth {
	compatible = "digi,rfkill-bt-cc";
	vin-supply = <&reg_rf_bt_en>;
	status = "okay";
};
